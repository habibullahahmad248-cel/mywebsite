ABOUT US

Welcome to Yashuni Agricultural Nigeria Limited, a premier agro-export company committed to showcasing the richness of Africa’s agricultural heritage to the global market.

Founded on the principles of quality, sustainability, and ethical trade, we pride ourselves on delivering premium agricultural products that meet and exceed international standards. Our mission is not only to provide exceptional products but also to create meaningful connections between African farmers and global consumers, fostering growth and mutual prosperity.

OUR HERITAGE

The name Yashuni embodies trust, resilience, and a legacy of excellence in the agricultural sector. Built on years of experience and a passion for agriculture, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for high-quality agro-exports.

From the golden grains of Africa’s fertile soils to the vibrant spices that define its cuisines, we aim to bring the continent’s agricultural treasures to the world while ensuring that the people behind the produce—our farmers—are empowered and rewarded fairly.
